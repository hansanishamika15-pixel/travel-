Culture & Heritage

Sri Lanka has a rich cultural tapestry shaped by Buddhism, colonial history and local traditions.

Ancient Cities & Monuments

Anuradhapura, Polonnaruwa and Sigiriya are key historical centers with stupas, palaces and rock art.

Festivals

  • Esala Perahera (Kandy) — Grand procession with dancers and decorated elephants (July/August).
  • Vesak — Celebrates the birth, enlightenment and death of the Buddha (May).
  • Sinhala & Tamil New Year — Mid-April celebrations, games and traditional food.

Arts & Crafts

Local arts include Kandyan dance, drumming, batik fabrics, mask carving and woodwork.

UNESCO World Heritage Sites

Sri Lanka hosts eight UNESCO sites including Sigiriya, Ancient City of Polonnaruwa and the Sacred City of Kandy.
