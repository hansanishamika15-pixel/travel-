Practical Travel Tips

Best Time to Visit

Sri Lanka has two monsoon seasons; the best time depends on which coast you plan to visit. Generally, December–April is ideal for the west and south; May–September for the east.

Health & Safety

  • Carry mosquito repellent; dengue is present in some areas.
  • Drink bottled water and avoid ice from uncertain sources.
  • Follow local advice for wildlife safaris and stay with guides.

Money & Communication

Currency: Sri Lankan Rupee (LKR). ATMs are common in cities; carry cash for rural areas. Purchase a local SIM card for data (Dialog, Mobitel, Airtel).

Transport

Trains in Sri Lanka offer scenic journeys (e.g., Kandy to Ella). Tuk-tuks are common for short trips; agree price beforehand or ensure meter is used.

Culture & Etiquette

  • Remove shoes and cover shoulders/legs when entering temples.
  • Use your right hand for giving/receiving items as a sign of respect.
  • Ask permission before photographing people, especially in rural communities.
